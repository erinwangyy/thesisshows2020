BFA and MFA work from art programs across America

Because of COVID-19, and the necessary closures of schools across the United States, many students were not able to install their annual thesis shows on campuses this school year. This was disappointing, and in many cases, devastating. We created this website, to serve as a location for the public to find links for 2020 BFA, and MFA thesis shows. If your school would like to participate, please get in touch.
