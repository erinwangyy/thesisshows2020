BFA and MFA work from art programs across America

Because of COVID-19, and the necessary closures of schools across the United States, many students were not able to install their annual thesis shows on campuses this school year. This was disappointing, and in many cases, devastating. We created this website, to serve as a location for the public to find links for the 2020 BFA, and MFA thesis work for art programs across America.
